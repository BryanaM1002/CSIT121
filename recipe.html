Blondies

Simply Recipes

  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 cup of dakr brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teapsoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of butterscotch chips
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Lightly butter and flour an 8x8-ich pan.
  3. Mix together the melted butter and sugar in a bowl.
  4. Add egg and vanilla extract and whisk to combine all.
  5. Add flour,baking soda, baking powder, salt, and butterscotch chips.
  6. Pour mixture into the pan and spread evenly then bake for 18 to 25 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool, cut into squares and serve.

Blondies have always been my favorite go to dessert, it's not extremely sweet like a dark chocolate brownie and it can perfectly be paired with vanilla icecream which is a lovely pair of hot and cold foods.
